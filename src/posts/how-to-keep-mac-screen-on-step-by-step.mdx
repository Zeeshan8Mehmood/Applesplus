## **How to Keep Mac Screen On - Steps**

Here in this article, we will explain How to Change Screen Timeout on Mac with the help of some easy and detailed steps. Let's start with our detailed steps.

### **Step 1: Click on Apple Logo**

If you look at your Mac screen, you will see the Apple logo on your Mac screen's upper left corner. Just click on the apple logo in the corner of your mac screen.

### **Step 2: Click on System Preferences**

After clicking on the Apple logo, a menu will open on your mac screen. On the 2nd row of the menu, you will see System Preferences, click on it.

### **Step 3: Click on Energy Saver**

After that step, another window will appear on your mac screen. Search Energy Saver in that window and click on it. The Energy Saver option will have a light bulb icon.

### **Step 4: Move Slider to Never**

After clicking the Energy Saver option, another window will appear on your Mac screen. Just move that slider to the end of the line and after this your Mac screen will keep on.

If you just want to increase or change your mac screen timeout, you can also change it for this menu. Just drag the same slider to any given time like 30 mins or 60 mins and it will fix your screen timeout on that place.

## **Conclusion**

In the above article, we discussed how to keep your mac screen on or how to chane screen timeout on mac. 

You can easily fix this issue with the help of the detailed upper steps within a few minutes.
