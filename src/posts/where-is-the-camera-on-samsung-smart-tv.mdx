  Smart TV is becoming more modern over time and today's smart TV comes with
  many modern features like built-in Wi-Fi and a built-in camera. In this
  article, we will see that where is the camera on Samsung smart TV. Samsung F
  Series Smart TVs come with a built-in camera. 


  On Samsung F Series Smart TV, we can see the camera on its top. It is an adjustable camera; when you need to use the camera, you can appear it on the top of your smart tv and when you don't need it, you can push it inside the TV case. 


  ### How to Use Samsung Smart TV Built-in Camera


  If you want to extend or appear your Samsung smart TV camera, we will show you how to do it. When you look at the top of the TV, you will see the camera button. Push that button and you will hear a click sound also. During pushing the camera button, kindly do not touch the camera lens. You can also check in the figure below that how to use Samsung TV built-in camera.


  ![Samsung Smart TV Built-in Camera](/img/1.png "Samsung Smart TV Camera")


  ### How to Turn off Samsung Smart TV Built-in Camera


  It is also pretty simple like it's activating. If you don't need a camera, you can push it, and it will go inside the TV case and you will hear the sound of a click, as you can see in the figure below. This step will turn off your smart tv camera, but its voice recognition feature will be working.


  ![Smart tv camera](/img/2.png "tv camera")


  **Please Note:**


  Only Samsung F Series Smart TVs come with built-in cameras. If you have any other model that does not come with a built-in camera, you will need to connect the external camera with your TV.


  ### Which Samsung TV has a built-in camera and built-in Mic


  There are four Samsung smart tv series with built-in cameras and built-in mics; simply 11 Samsung smart TVs have built-in cameras and mic. The Samsung plasma series has three models, which come with a built-in camera and mic.


  The Samsung 700 LCD LED has three models, which come with a built-in camera and mic. Samsung 8000 also has three models, and the Samsung 9000 has only one model nowadays, which also comes with built-in cameras.
